> MISSION BRIEF
Claude Code displays 187 unique spinner verbs while processing tasks.
Your mission: catch them all.
> HOW TO HUNT
Verbs cycle randomly on the center of the screen.
See one you want? Press SPACE or click [ CATCH ] to grab it.
Each verb can only be caught once. Duplicates are ignored.
> RARITY SYSTEM
COMMON — Standard verbs, green glow
RARE — Uncommon verbs, magenta glow, 26 total
LEGENDARY — Mythic verbs, golden glow, only 10 exist
> SPEED MODES
1 NORMAL — 0.8s cycle, brisk baseline
2 FAST — 0.5s cycle, reflex training
3 INSANE — 0.3s cycle, ultimate challenge
> MISSIONS — 4 MODES
Press M to open the mission select.
01 CLASSIC — Catch all 187. Untimed. Zen hunt.
02 KERNEL PANIC — SYS drains 3%/s. Commons poison, rares stabilise, legendaries hot-patch. Catch 3 LEGENDARY before 0%.
03 REGEX GAUNTLET — A live /regex/ rotates every 10s. Matching catches score big; non-matches cost you.
04 FORK BOMB — :(){ :|:& };:. The spinner forks at 60/45/30s. Keys 1–4 focus, SPACE catches; heat = panic.
> KEYBINDINGS
SPACE Catch the current verb
C Toggle collection panel
M Open mission select
B Toggle ambient BGM
S Enter screensaver mode
R Retry current mission (on results screen)
H Toggle this help panel
ESC Close any open panel
> AUDIO
A looping cyberpunk ambient track is synthesized on the fly — no external files, just Web Audio oscillators.
Click ♪ in the header or press B to toggle.
> SCREENSAVER
Press S or click SCR.SAVER to take over the screen with a drifting verb storm, random codex flashes and scrolling transmission tickers.
Any key or click resumes the hunt.
> DATA PERSISTENCE
Your collection is auto-saved to localStorage.
Close the page and come back — your progress stays intact.
